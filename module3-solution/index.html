Chicken
Chicken is believed to be extremely beneficial to the human body. But, like any product, there are negative properties of chicken consumption. Today we will tell you all about the benefits and harms of chicken. The composition of chicken meat Chicken is considered an ideal dietary product. It's all about its low calorie and high protein content. So, for 100 grams of chicken there are only 113 calories, 23.6 grams of protein, 1.9 grams of fat and 0.4 grams of carbohydrates. There are about 70 grams of water in chicken.Chicken is rich in various vitamins, micro - and macronutrients. Yes, it contains vitamins PP, A, B1, B2, B5, B6, B9 and B12, as well as C, E, N. Chicken meat has a high content of potassium, sulfur, fluoride, and a lot of calcium. magnesium, sodium, phosphorus, chlorine, iodine, iron, zinc, copper, manganese and cobalt. It is the composition that determines the health benefits of chicken meat. We will tell in more detail about advantage further. The benefits of chicken In addition to the composition, the benefit is influenced by the general method of their cultivation. Therefore, domestic chickens raised in spacious cages and with a variety of foods are much more useful than factory. As a rule, the latter are stuffed with compound feed and growth stimulants, which is clearly not beneficial. However, they still have beneficial properties for human health. First of all, chicken meat contains a huge amount of complete animal protein, which benefits the body in terms of building tissues and muscles. The chicken contains all the necessary amino acids, a set of cartilaginous proteins and a building material for tissues. Chicken legs, which contain collagen and elastin, have incredible benefits. Consumption of these parts of chicken will help people suffering from joint diseases.
